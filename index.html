Licenses

I currently hold licenses for the FINRA Securities Industry Essentials (SIE) exam , FINRA General Securities Representative (Series 7) exam , and the FINRA Securities Trader Representative (Series 57) exam. These tests are administered by FINRA, the Financial Industry Regulatory Authority, in order to gauge a test applicant's understanding of financial markets and regulations in the United States.
